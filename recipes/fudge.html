Recipe: Vegan Chocolate Peanut Butter Fudge

Description

This vegan chocolate peanut butter fudge takes only a few ingredients and a few minutes to put together. It's super easy to make and tastes delicious while being healthier than most fudge recipes.

This fudge is comforting and delicious, and is family approved too!

Ingredients

  • 1/3 cup peanut butter, unsalted (85 g)
  • 3/4 cup chocolate chips (130 g)
  • 1-2 tbsp maple syrup
  • 1 tbsp peanut butter, unsalted

Steps

  1. Add peanut butter to a saucepan and melt over low heat, stirring constantly.
  2. Add in chocolate chips and agave syrup or maple syrup and stir until everything is melted and well-combined.
  3. Transfer the mixture to a lined small baking dish or a glass container and spread evenly with a spatula.
  4. Add small teaspoons of melted peanut butter on top and create swirls on the surface with a knife.
  5. Place in the fridge for 1 hour or until set. Cut into squares and enjoy!
